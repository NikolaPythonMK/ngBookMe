How to Reset Your Password

If you've forgotten your password or need to reset it for any reason, follow these steps to regain access to your BookingApp account:

Step 1: Access the Settings

Click on your profile icon located on the top right of the navigation bar. This will open a menu with various options

Step 2: Navigate to "Settings and Privacy"

From the menu, select "Settings and Privacy." This will take you to the settings page where you can manage your account preferences.

Step 3: Reset Your Password

On the settings page, locate the "Password" or "Security" section. You may need to scroll down to find it.

Step 4: Enter Your Current Password (if prompted)

For security reasons, you may be asked to enter your current password before proceeding. If prompted, enter your current password.

Step 5: Create a New Password

Next, you'll have the option to create a new password. Follow these guidelines:

  • Choose a strong password that includes a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
  • Make sure your new password is unique and not easily guessable.
  • Remember to write down or securely store your new password for future reference.
Step 6: Confirm Your New Password

Confirm your new password by entering it again in the provided field.

Step 7: Save Changes

Once you've entered and confirmed your new password, click the "Save" or "Update Password" button to save the changes.

Step 8: Password Successfully Reset

Congratulations! Your password has been successfully reset. You can now use your new password to log in to your BookingApp account.
