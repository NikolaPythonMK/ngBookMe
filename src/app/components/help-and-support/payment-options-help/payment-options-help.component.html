What are the Payment Options?

BookingApp offers various payment methods to make your booking process convenient and secure. Here are the payment options available:

1. Credit or Debit Card

You can pay for your bookings using major credit or debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Simply enter your card details during the checkout process to complete your payment.

2. PayPal

If you prefer using PayPal, you can link your PayPal account to BookingApp and make payments securely through your PayPal balance or linked bank account or credit card.

3. Mobile Wallets

We also accept payments from popular mobile wallets, such as Apple Pay and Google Pay. Simply select your preferred mobile wallet option during checkout and follow the prompts to complete your payment.

4. Online Banking

For added convenience, BookingApp supports online banking payments. You can transfer funds directly from your bank account to complete your booking.

5. Other Payment Methods

Depending on your location and the property or service you're booking, additional payment methods may be available, such as bank transfers, cash payments, or alternative payment providers.

Please note that available payment options may vary based on your region and the specific property or service you're booking. Payment security is a top priority for BookingApp, and all transactions are encrypted and processed securely.
