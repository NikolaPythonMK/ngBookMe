How Can I Cancel or Modify a Booking?

If you need to cancel a booking on BookingApp, you can do so easily through your profile's "Bookings" section. Follow these steps:

Step 1: Log In

Ensure you're logged in to your BookingApp account. If you haven't logged in yet, please do so now.

Step 2: Access Your Bookings

Click on your profile icon located on the top right of the navigation bar. From the dropdown menu, select "Bookings." This will take you to a list of your current and past reservations.

Step 3: Find the Booking to Cancel

Scroll through your list of bookings and find the one you wish to cancel. Click on the booking to view its details.

Step 4: Cancel Your Booking

On the booking details page, you'll find a "Delete Reservation" or "Cancel Booking" button. Click this button to initiate the cancellation process.

Step 5: Confirmation

Confirm your decision to cancel the booking. Depending on our cancellation policy, you may be eligible for a refund or subject to cancellation fees.

That's it! Your booking has been successfully canceled. Any applicable refunds or charges will be processed according to our cancellation policy.

Step 1: Contact Support

If you encounter any issues or have concerns regarding your booking, the first step is to get in touch with our dedicated support team.

Step 2: Reach Out

You can contact our support team through the following methods:

  • Send an email to [support email address].
  • Call our support hotline at [support phone number].
  • Use the live chat feature on our website or app, if available.
Step 3: Provide Details

When contacting support, please provide as much detail as possible about the issue you're facing:

  • Your booking reference number (if available).
  • The nature of the problem (e.g., incorrect dates, payment issues, accommodation concerns).
  • Any relevant screenshots or documentation to help us understand the issue better.
Step 4: Resolution

Our support team will work diligently to resolve the issue as quickly as possible. Depending on the nature of the problem, this may involve:

  • Rebooking your reservation with corrected details.
  • Processing refunds, if applicable and in line with our policies.
  • Assisting with alternative accommodations or solutions if needed.
