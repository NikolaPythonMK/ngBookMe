How to Make a Booking

If you're ready to make a booking on BookingApp, follow these simple steps:

Step 1: Log In

Ensure you're logged in to your BookingApp account. If you haven't logged in yet, please do so now.

Step 2: Navigate to "Post Property"

You can access the booking section in two ways:

  • Click on the "Post Property" link located in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
  • Alternatively, you can click the "Post Property" button within your profile, specifically in the "Posted Properties" section.
Step 3: Provide Booking Details

Once you're on the booking page, you'll need to provide the necessary information for your booking:

  • Choose the date and time for your booking.
  • Select any additional options or preferences.
Step 4: Confirm and Submit

Review the details of your booking to ensure everything is accurate. If everything looks good, click the "Submit" or "Book" button to confirm your booking.

Step 5: Confirmation

Once your booking is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email or notification with all the relevant details.
